Brindavan Garden & KRS Dam

  • Mysore's natural beauty is another aspect that adds to its allure. The Brindavan Gardens, situated adjacent to the Krishnarajasagara Dam, is a splendid horticultural delight with its well-manicured lawns, vibrant flowerbeds, and mesmerizing musical fountain shows. The city is surrounded by lush greenery, with the nearby Bandipur National Park and Nagarhole National Park offering wildlife enthusiasts a chance to encounter diverse flora and fauna.
