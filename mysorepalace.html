Mysore Palace

  • Mysore, officially known as Mysuru is a vibrant and enchanting city located in the southern part of the Indian state of Karnataka.
.

  • Mysore is renowned for its majestic palaces, with the iconic Mysore Palace being the crown jewel.Beyond the palace, Mysore is adorned with numerous other architectural gems. The Jaganmohan Palace, known for its beautiful paintings and art gallery, and the Lalitha Mahal, which is now a luxurious heritage hotel, are worth exploring. The city also houses the Jayalakshmi Vilas Mansion, a magnificent structure that serves as the campus for the University of Mysore, and the Rajendra Vilas Palace, which now houses the International School of Information Management.
