Chamundi Hill & Devaraja Market

  • The Chamundeshwari Temple, situated atop the Chamundi Hills, is a revered place of worship and offers a breathtaking view of the city below.
  • The city's vibrant markets, such as the Devaraja Market, are a treat for the senses. Here, one can experience the bustling atmosphere, vibrant colors, and aromatic spices that define the essence of Mysore. The city is famous for its silk sarees, intricate woodwork, sandalwood products, and delicious Mysore Pak, a sweet delicacy that is a must-try for visitors.
